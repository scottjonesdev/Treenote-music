News

January 8, 2022

Name & Music Distribution Changes

After much contemplation, I have decided to released my music under a monikor rather than my real name. Going forward my music will now be released under the name Treenote.

Furthermore, I have decided to limit my music distribution to Bandcamp and YouTube. Bandcamp offers by far the best model for artists and aren't investing in weapons tech like some other streaming services…

In an effort to keep my music accessible, I have enabled unlimited streaming on Bandcamp for my albums and lowered the price to 'pay what you want'. Also, my music will remain available to stream via my YouTube channel.

June 13, 2021

I released my new album 'Alone' on Friday the 9th of June. Thematically, the title is an allusion to the process of making ambient electronic music, and being ‘alone’ with my machines. 'Alone' is available to stream via YouTube or download via Bandcamp.
